Special Occasion

When choosing an activity for that bridal shower, birthday party or girls night, it's important to have fun and make memories. You can enjoy your party at home with a few drinks or at the park with cake and ballons and we will bring the activity to you. $25 non-refundable deposit. Total cost based on items of your choosing and amount of painters.

Nursing Home/Assisted Living

This is a unique and fun way to get your residents at assisted livings or nursing homes involved and busy using their sensory skills. The activity usually takes about an hour to build and all.

Fundraiser/Events

Pick us for your next fundraiser or event and let everyone come together with creativity. Everything from Veteran's day to 4-H clubs and Christmas parties. A great way to raise money and involve the community. $25 non-refundable deposit. Total cost based on items of your choosing and amount of painters.

About Us

We bring the activity to you! Encouraging you to use your imagination and painting skills. We arrive with a variety of plain bisque and paint. You then simply paint your piece however you desire and we will deliver your finished product after it has been glazed and fired.
